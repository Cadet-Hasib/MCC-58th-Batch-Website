About Mirzapur Cadet College



History Of Mirzapur Cadet College


The Mirzapur Cadet College was established on 29th November. It is the 3rd cadet College of Bangladesh. Every year 50 bright students from all across the country compete to get the chance to study in here.



East Pakistan period


The Mirzapur Cadet College was established on 29 November 1963. Thus it was established before the Liberation war of 1971. The first principal of MCC was Micheal William Pitt.



Liberation War Period

During the Liberation war, Bangabandhu Sheikh Mujibur Rahman gave the speech to the whole bengle on 7th March 1971. The possesion had a great effect on the college on the 8thof March. The then principal send all to thier own home. All became normal in the month of December.



Bangladesh Period



The foundation stone


Every year many students from all across Bangladesh give the admission exam to get admitted to Cadet Cadet. Only 600 students ultimately get the chance to studying in Cadet College. At present there are 12 Cadet Colleges in Bangladesh. In which 9 are for boys and 3 are for girls. MCC is on of the best cadet College in Bangladesh.



About MCC 58th Batch

The MCC 58th batch is the newest batch of the year 2020. But Due to the Pandemic Covid-19 the cadets of 58th Batch are not able to go to the college. Therefore they are getting education through electronics. The newest batch of cadets will soon be gathered and dream for the best for the future.
